About

Xilin Tang is a Phd student at Department of Computer Science at Cornell University, primarily based at the NYC campus of Cornell Tech, working with Prof. Alex Conway. He has also closely collaborated with Profs. Huanchen Zhang, Feng Zhang, and Shuhao Zhang.
His research focuses on quantitatively and formally understanding system behaviors and leveraging this understanding to enhance practical efficiency with theoretical assurance.
